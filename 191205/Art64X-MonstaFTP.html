MonstaFTP

How to upload files to your new domain if you do not have a FTP program

  1. Go to http://www.monstaftp.com
  2. Click on "demo" button
  3. On the resulting screen, click on the link to the FTP demo
  4. On the resulting demo streen, enter your ftp info (hostname, username, password).
